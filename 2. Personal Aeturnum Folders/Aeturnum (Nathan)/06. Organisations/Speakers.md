A council of representatives of all the different groups, organisations, guilds, and peoples of [[2. Personal Aeturnum Folders/Aeturnum (Vanessa)/Bloodtide/Adrestia]], formed in the wake of [[2. Personal Aeturnum Folders/Aeturnum (Nathan)/06. Organisations/The Nine]] falling silent after Elias Jassen's death. 

Members:
- Lenore - Speaker of the Occultists
- Lady Zarissa - Speaker of the Undercity
- Vivian Anelin - Speaker of Typhon Foundry

## Lenore

Current Status: Unknown

Speaker of the Occultists and chief researcher of the [[Worldwound]]. Ally of [[Elaric Valent]] and [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|The Riftborn]] during the events of Aeturnum 1. Experienced an existential crisis upon discovering herself to essentially be character written by Jacquetta Iverson.

## Lady Zarissa

Current Status: Unknown

Speaker of the Undercity and former mob boss of the Syndicate crime organisation. Ally of the Riftborn during the events of Aeturnum 1. Since becoming Speaker, she has lifted up the Undercity, which is now prospering, as of the events of [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude)|Genesis]].  

# Vivian Anelin

Current Status: Unknown, possibly deceased (may have been onboard the [[Technology|Phospherian Five]] when it was destroyed by Haera)

Speaker (and owner) of Typhon Foundry. Conducts R&D on new [[Technology|technology and weapons]]. Minor antagonist of the Riftborn during the events of Aeturnum 1.
