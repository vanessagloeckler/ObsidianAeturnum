# Basics

Theolyn Esmond was a member of the [[Wayfarer’s Guild NS]], and friend of the PCs. During the events of the Requiem Expedition, he and his squad were captured, tortured, enthralled, and then all killed except for Theolyn himself. Theolyn's body had been mutilated in order to make him resemble an angel - the significance of this is unknown. 

The party discovered Theolyn in the torture chamber that exists in between Aernoc Castle and the Foundry, where he had been horribly mutilated, to the point where he could not be saved. He was also close to the point of turning into a bloodkin or some other such creature. After a brief exchange with [[Orinoco NS]] and [[Valkath NS]], Orinoco used radiant magic to attempt to banish the thrall and put Theolyn to rest.

While exiting the torture chamber, [[Milo NS]] found a photo of himself with Theolyn, who was holding [[01. Questions NS#The Wayfarer's Guild|a baby]].
