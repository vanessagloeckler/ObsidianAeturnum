# Backstory, up to the events of Genesis

Elaric Valent was the [[First Blade & First Shield|First Blade]] of Adrestia during the events of Aeturnum 1, and a major player in the Masquerade narrative arc. 

He was raised in an academy, along with his sister Heloise, that officially sought out to create powerful and heroic champions to defend Adrestia. In actuality, the academy experimented on its children, augmenting them with arcane and paracausal forces in an attempt to create metahuman entities. Both Elaric and Heloise were turned into some manner of [[Vampirism|Half Vampire]] entity, gaining powerful combat abilities and magic, transformative abilities, and thirst for blood/flesh, while remaining resistant to sunlight and other detriments of vampirism. Heloise died as a result of these experiments, but unknown to Elaric, her consciousness became implanted inside his subconscious, as Elaric was ruled to be more obedient than Heloise. 

At some point between this and the events of Masquerade, Elaric arrived in Adrestia and rose to the rank of 'First Blade' - a largely ceremonial title denoting him as Adrestia's most powerful warrior and a commander of the military. He also took on a ward named [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|Tia]], a Tiefling woman and member of the Mechanist's Guild. Elaric and Tia developed some manner of close emotional connection, though it was never established if this was romantic or platonic. 

During the events of Masquerade, Elaric assisted in the battle against the Hierophant, but developed a second personality in the form of Heloise, who was loyal to the Hierophant. Heloise was capable of transforming into an entity known as The Pursuer, who was a secondary antagonist of the Riftborn. 

During the events of the Nightfall narrative event, Elaric led a large force of Adrestian soldiers - infiltrated by the Riftborn with Elaric's assistance - to a town that was falling into the Astral Corridor. It was a trap set up by The Hierophant, and many were killed, including Tia. All that died there were consumed by the Astral Corridor, resulting in them being erased from history, and the memories of everyone except the Riftborn. Elaric forgot Tia, but was aware of some strange void in his life, as a result.

With time rewritten, Elaric's life was altered. In this new reality, he was betrothed to Rosaria, the recently appointed Keeper of Adrestia, in a purely political marriage to strengthen ties between Adrestia and Rosaria's home. The wedding took place during the events of Ever After, where Rosaria was exposed as an ally of The Hierophant called the Shrike, and Elaric became aware of the Heloise parasite living inside him. Rosaria escaped, but was unable to stop the Riftborn from separating Elaric and Heloise, convincing Heloise to return Elaric's body back to him.

Elaric returned to the battle against the Hierophant, and was instrumental in bringing her down. He defended the Riftborn after the events of Ever After, when he was trapped in a time loop battle against Rosaria - winning, losing, and dying repeatedly in order to prevent Rosaria from killing the unconscious Riftborn. He suffered great injuries, and experienced a similar fading away that the Riftborn were going through, despite not being Riftborn himself. Elaric survived the end of Masquerade, though his health is uncertain. He remained in Adrestia and became known as a mentor to many of the heroes on the ill-fated [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude)|Genesis Expedition]]. He either lost or gave up his title as First Blade, which was passed onto [[Other NPCs|Cassandra]], a former Guild Master. At some point prior to Genesis, Elaric disappeared, and his present whereabouts was unknown.

Elaric was AFAB, but transitioned during his teenage years, with the help of his sister.
