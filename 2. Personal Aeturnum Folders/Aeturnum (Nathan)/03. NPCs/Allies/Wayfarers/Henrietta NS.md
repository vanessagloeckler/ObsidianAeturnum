# Meta Notes

Henrietta is inspired by/named after Gilli's cat.
# Basics

**Species:** Machine, possibly something else previously
**Pronouns:** Unknown, Scarlet refers to Henrietta as 'her'
**Status:** Alive/Operational
**Location:** Unknown, assumedly with [[Scarlet NS]] in the Wayfarer's Convoy

What exactly Henrietta is, and whether it is a sentient entity, is unknown, but the machine may be controlled by some sort of creature inside the tank.
# Appearances
- [[0.2 Requiem NS]]
# Physical Appearance

Henrietta is a 9ft. tall [[Technology NS#Automata|Typhon Automata]] with the addition of a [[Technology NS|coffin-like tank]], that appears to contain something/someone.  
# Personality

While it is unknown whether Henrietta is sentient, it demonstrates some amount of personality. It shows affection towards Scarlet and Beetle, exhibits fear in response to an illusory image of Haera Agelamorn created by Abyssian, and 'chirps happily' while killing bloodkin.
# Connection to the Wayfarers

Henrietta appears to be a member of the [[Wayfarer’s Guild NS]], though the exact association is unclear.

Henrietta is particularly fond of [[Beetle NS]].
# Connection to other NPCs

[[Scarlet NS]] - Henrietta seems to be a companion of Scarlet.
