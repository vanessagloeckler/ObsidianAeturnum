# Physical Appearance

Artificial red hair, cut short and jagged, as though cut with a knife. Typically wears leather armour.
# Personality

Scarlet seems abrasive and projects an aura of disregard for her allies, but her actions suggest a deeper concern. During the events of Requiem, she and Henrietta placed themselves in front of a horde of bloodkin in order to give her squad more time to escape.
# Connection to the Wayfarers

She is a commander of the [[Wayfarer’s Guild]].

[[Abyssian]] used detect thoughts on Scarlet and discovered some deeper nature to her relationship with Elaric.
# Connection to other NPCs

[[Astra]] - It seems that Scarlet doesn't like going near Astra, and Astra believes that Scarlet has been stealing lyria to sell.
[[Elaric Valent]] - As Guild Master, Elaric is her commanding officer. On the surface, she seems to dislike him, but there seems to be some deeper connection. 
[[Henrietta]] - Seems to have a close bond, fights together
