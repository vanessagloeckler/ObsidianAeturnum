# Mina

Met during the events of [[1.4 Anathema|Anathema]], Mina is a [[Vampirism|vampire]], and former member of [[Haera Agelamorn]]'s court. She left Tisis a long time ago, and was vocally opposed to Haera even before the opening of the Worldwound. She came to Adrestia to research the [[Petrification Curse]] and to try and heal the sick.
