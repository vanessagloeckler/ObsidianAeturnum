# Bloodkin

Described as grotesque monsters with too many teeth. They are created when a humanoid is placed into a [[Technology|coffin-like tank]] and pumped full of vampiric blood.

Creatures called Bloodkin also featured heavily in [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|Bloodtide]]. Similarities currently unknown.

AC: 13
HP: Less than 18
Movement: 30ft
Weaknesses: Fire, possibly radiant

Can inflict the [[04. Game Mechanics|Bleed & Haemorrhage]] condition.
# Warden & Witch

A ogre-like armoured creature (Warden), who held a shrieking undead corpse (Witch) chained to a poll. They moved as one and acted as one, but had two separate ACs and hit points.

The Warden was a melee combatant that could impose his shield to protect the witch from damage, as well as use powerful attacks that could throw opponents.

The Witch was a spellcaster, capable of;
- Creating a shield to block the sun
- Counterspell (a specific ability, not the standard spell)
