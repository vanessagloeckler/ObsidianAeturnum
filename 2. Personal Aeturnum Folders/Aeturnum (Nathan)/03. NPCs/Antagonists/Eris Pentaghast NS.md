Eris Pentaghast is an Adrestian warrior of significant power and renown, given that she claimed the title of [[Adrestia NS#First Blade|First Blade]]. She was responsible for selecting all the members of the failed [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude) NS|Genesis expedition]], and accompanied them on it.

During the battle against [[Haera Agelamorn NS|Haera Agelamorn]], she was responsible for controlling the [[Technology NS#The Diluxium Sphere|Diluxium Sphere]] that was on board the Phospherian Five, until both were destroyed by Haera's Blood Lance ability. 

When the last of the Adrestian force was killed, Haera took Eris Pentaghast captive, torturing her to near-death and reviving her by force-feeding her her own flesh. This went on for a period of months, breaking Eris's mind.

Eris appears to now be a still-living thrall of Haera.
