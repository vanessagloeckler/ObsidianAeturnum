# Cassandra

Current Status: Unknown
Species: Unknown

An ally of the Riftborn and the [[2. Personal Aeturnum Folders/Aeturnum (Vanessa)/Bloodtide/Adrestia|Justicar's Guild]] leader during the events of Aeturnum 1.

At some point after Aeturnum 1, she became the [[First Blade & First Shield|First Blade]], assumedly succeeding Elaric. However, she vacated this position prior to the events of [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude)|Genesis]], and was succeeded by [[Eris Pentaghast]].
# Ves Talshen

Current Status: Unknown
Species: Tiefling - Possibly half Tiefling (Mottled blue skin)

Minor antagonist of the Riftborn during the events of Aeturnum 1, and sister of Kastia Talshen, an Eidolon operative and major player of Aeturnum 1. She was last seen during the introduction of [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude)|Genesis]], where she was the assistant of [[Speakers|Speaker Zarissa]]. She feigned ignorance when [[Victims of the Genesis Expedition|Loup Garou]] tried to identify her (above table note: David couldn't be direct about it as his character wouldn't know the significance of Ves's presence).  

#### Events of Aeturnum 1:
She was separated from/abandoned by Kastia as a child, and fell into a life of crime, fighting, and drugs in the [[2. Personal Aeturnum Folders/Aeturnum (Vanessa)/Bloodtide/Adrestia|Adrestian Undercity]]. She made an enemy of Speaker Zarissa, who was at the time the Syndicate crime boss. She eventually crossed paths with the Hierophant, who abducted and brainwashed her into fighting against Eidolon.
