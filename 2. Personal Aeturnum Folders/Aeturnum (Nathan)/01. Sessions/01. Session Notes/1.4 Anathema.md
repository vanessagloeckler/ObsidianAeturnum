# Session Start

It is mid-morning, and we are at the [[Wayfarer’s Guild|Wayfarer's Convoy]]. Around us, we can hear the sounds of people training, knives and forks scraping against plates, wayfarers going about their daily lives, but beneath all the casual noise, we can hear the sounds of our allies crying out as they slowly succumb to the [[Curseblood Plague]]. Silas Reiner, a Lieutenant of the guild, approaches us. He is a tall human (assumed) man, with black hair, brown skin and eyes, dressed relatively formally with a white shirt beneath his leather armour. He informs us that we have a visitor and a mission.

Lilah Willowbrook, Speaker for an unknown faction of Adrestia approaches - she is a halfling woman with blue eyes, wearing a formal blue dress. She is seeking assistance regarding the [[Petrification Curse]], which is running rampant through Adrestia. She tells us that a significant number of people within [[Adrestia|Brigmore Commons]] have been impacted, and that there have been reports of people claiming to be cured by an unknown alchemist. The alchemist described as feminine, slim, with blonde hair, and eyes that change from brown to red. Orinoco points out that Lilah has just provided a fairly accurate description of [[Haera Agelamorn]]. Lilah dismisses this possibility, but says the alchemist is assumed to be a vampire, and a former member of the [[Tisis|Court of Tisis]]. Given that this unknown vampire appears to be trying to help, Lilah requests that we identify and find them in order to enlist their aid, but not to kill them.

Ember suggests that we could take food and medical supplies with us to enable smoother information gathering. Lilah says she has already given all that she can, which Ember insights as being truthful, but that Lilah feels deep shame about it. We gather supplies from the wayfarers convoy, consisting mainly of food, and Lilah directs us to the *Sun Above Water* tavern, which has been converted into a care centre in the wake of the curse. Silas announces that he will accompany us, Lilah tells us to 'remember our vow' - the Worldwound must be closed, and Haera must die - and we head towards Brigmore Common.
