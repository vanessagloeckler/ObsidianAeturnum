# Dream Sequence

We each experience a similar nightmare, submerged in the coffin-like tanks that we encountered in the harvesting chamber during the events of [[0.2 Requiem]]. We feel a 'maelstrom of blood' around us as we feel ourselves go pale, transforming, experiencing a deep hunger.
# Session Start

We each awake from our nightmares in the late morning. We are in a large tent within the [[Wayfarer’s Guild|convoy]]. Distressed by their dreams, several of us have bad awakenings, including Elias trampling Beetle, and Brick punching Orinoco in the face. Scarlet arrives, dressed in the black and gold uniform of the guild. She repeatedly mocks us for having lost our memories while refusing to take responsibility for her role in that, while informing us that the [[Elaric Valent|Guild Master]] doesn't believe we're ready to return to active duty - though Scarlet herself disagrees. As such, we're to be tested by hunting [[Haera's Forces|bloodkin]].

We leave our tent and see the convoy for the first time - colossal metal wagons, each one pulled by multiple horses, with heavy armour plating and weapons. The front of the convoy features the most heavily defended wagons, and Scarlet refers to it as the vanguard. The wagons look modular, with the capacity to join together and have extra parts added. We are on the outskirts of [[Adrestia]] and can see the city in the distance, as well as valleys and forests closer by. Some of the trees in the forest have begun to [[Petrification Curse|petrify]], and there is a significant amount of ash all around. As we move through the convoy, some wayfarers seem happy to see us, while others avoid us, seeming scared or angry.
