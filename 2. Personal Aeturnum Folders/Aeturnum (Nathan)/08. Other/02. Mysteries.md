# The Forgotten

During a mental conversation between Astra and Loup Garou in [[0.3 Initiation|Initiation]], Astra makes reference to 'The Forgotten'. However, Loup fails a WIS save and immediately forgets the phrase.

This may be [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|The Riftborn.]]
