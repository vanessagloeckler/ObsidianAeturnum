A curse related to the [[Worldwound NS|Worldwound]], that causes people to slowly turn to stone. 

During the events of [[0.1. Genesis (Prelude) NS|Genesis]], [[Victims of the Genesis Expedition NS#Mayday|Mayday]] discovered that statues found at [[Tisis NS#Aernoc Palace|Aernoc Palace]] contained still organic, still living material contained within the statues, and hypnotized that the purpose of the curse was to essentially create 'lunchboxes' of blood for the vampires.

However, it was also discovered that vampires themselves were coming under the effect of the curse, including members of the [[Tisis NS|Tisis Court]], seemingly all of whom had been executed by [[Haera Agelamorn NS|Haera Agelamorn]], though unclear on whether this was intentional, as it is not known if Haera intentionally created the Worldwound.

Based on the peaceful or excited expressions on the Tisis courtiers, they either welcomed the petrification, or else didn't know it was going to happen and were hit instantly by it.
