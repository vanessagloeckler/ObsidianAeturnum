Information on the exact nature of vampires is unclear. There may be several different types with different origins.

Known vampires:
- [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|Ashe]] - Thrall of unknown origin - Assumed deceased
- [[Elaric Valent]] - Half Vampire - Leader of the [[Wayfarer’s Guild]]
- [[Haera Agelamorn]] - Pure Vampire - Tisis
- Heloise Valent - Half Vampire - Deceased
- [[Adrestia NPCs|Mina]] - Thrall of unknown origin - Adrestia
- The [[Five Lieutenants]] - Thralls of Haera - Tisis
- The court of [[Tisis]] - Thralls of Haera - Deceased

Types of vampires:
- During the events of Aeturnum 1, there was a type of vampire that was created as a result of a science experiment.
- During the events of Aeturnum 1, there were vampires on [[05. Glossary of Minor Terms|Lumeria]], which were connected to [[03. Elements from Aeturnum 1|The Witness]]. An entity was contained within The Witness's Iron Maiden that was some description of 'First Vampire' or 'God of vampires', possibly the [[The Outer God of Blood]].
- Haera Agelamorn may be a third type of vampire.

Thrall - Created by another vampire - Must consume blood to survive
Half Vampire - Created by experimentation
Pure Vampire - Origins unclear, possibly the O.G. vampire

Mental state: Vampires are not predisposed to become evil, but do need to feed on the blood of living people. 

Weakness:
- Sunlight (half vampires like Elaric are unaffected)
