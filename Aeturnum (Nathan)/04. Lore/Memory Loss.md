When we woke up in the [[Tisis|Foundry]], we had lost all of our memories except for one core memory each.

It may be that we will be able to recover our memories with the passing of time. Unclear.

The way NPCs reacted to us having lost our memories suggests that it is not considered common (as opposed to the memory loss the Riftborn experienced in campaign 1).
# PC Memories

[[Abyssian]]: Working as an astrologer, and studying the Worldwound. He looked deep into the wound, and there he found his power.

[[Beetle]]: Chased away from his village 'for being helpful'. He was an alchemist, and brewed potions to heal people, but the potions often had unintended side effects such as turning people blue. He insists that he always made people healthier and never hurt anyone.

[[Galahad]]: Core memory is being sat driving one of the wagons of the Convoy, the smoke from a tarry cheroot stinging his eyes as the rest of the Wayfarers go about their business untroubled. (He forgot that he smoked until he saw Trystane smoking)

[[Marcello Gangrene]]: What felt like a recent memory, with intense deja vu. Sitting by a fire in a tavern, feeling safe and content, yet also lonely. 

[[Milo]]: Training in a courtyard with friends, sparring, teamwork, and comradery. 

[[Orinoco]]: Walking along a river with her mother as a child, and her mother telling her the meaning of 'Orinoco' - It means "The place where we go paddling together."

[[Valkath]]: Lost in a forest for days, starving. She hunted and killed a deer, mourning and honouring its sacrifice. She now wears the deer's antlers as tribute.

[[Wally]]: Childhood, and a dark memory of becoming separated from their mother.
