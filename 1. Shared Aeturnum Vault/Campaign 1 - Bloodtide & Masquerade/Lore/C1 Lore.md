# Astral Corridor

An extra-dimensional space where the laws of physics don't apply, that exists between realities. Access to the corridor was controlled by the Keeper of Adrestia, who drew their power from it. The Hierophant, and the witches of Lumeria also drew their power from it.

The [[#Riftborn]] would pass through the astral corridor, having been pulled there from their own worlds by the power of [[#The Witness]]. 

After the events of Aeturnum 1, access to the Astral Corridor has seemingly been sealed off. 
# Cult of the Formless Mind

An antagonist organisation during the events of Masquerade, that was run by [[C1 Antagonists#The Hierophant|The Hierophant]].
# Eidolon

An off-the-books black ops organisation, staffed primarily by Riftborn and charged with investigating and stopping the Cult of the Formless Mind, and the Hierophant.

All PCs were members of Eidolon during the events of Masquerade.
# The Keeper of Adrestia

A position held by at least ten people over the hundreds/thousands of years that Adrestia has stood. A power magic wielder, the Keeper is inherently linked to the Astral Corridor, and can wield paracausal abilities as a result. 

As of the end of Aeturnum 1, and the closing of the Astral Corridor, this position most likely no longer exists. 

Known holders of the title;
- 1 to 6: Unknown
- Seventh Keeper: Elizabeth Hall
- Eighth Keeper unknown
- Ninth Keeper: Lucille Duras
- Tenth Keeper: Rosaria
# The Nine

Nine wizards of phenomenal power who have transcended death and have dominion over all life in The Red Isles.

The only confirmed member of The Nine is [[C1 Antagonists#Elias Jassen|Elias Jassen]].
# The Promise

An alternate plane of reality that contains great power, sought by both [[C1 Antagonists#Elias Jassen|Elias Jassen]] and [[C1 Antagonists#The Hierophant|The Hierophant]].
# Riftborn

Powerful and (usually) heroic entities that had been torn from their worlds at the point of their deaths, and brought through the Astral Corridor to Adrestia by The Witness/Jacquetta Iverson. All Aeturnum 1 PCs were Riftborn. With the end of Aeturnum 1, all Riftborn vanished from the world, returned to their own realities. 

**META NOTE:** It is currently unclear whether the existence of the Riftborn is remembered by anyone other than [[Wayfarer NPCs#Elaric Valent|Elaric Valent]]. 
# The Witness

A God-like entity with dominion over death, responsible for bringing the Riftborn to this world. It was created by a fragment of Jacquetta Iverson's power, which [[C1 Antagonists#Elias Jassen|Elias Jassen]] manipulated for his own benefit. It had some connection to the Astral corridor, and to the vampires of Lumeria. 

Two common symbols were associated with The Witness; a figure in a pale shroud, and a sarcophagus/iron maiden. 

It was destroyed at the end of Aeturnum 1.
