Most of these characters served as 'avatar' characters for the Aeturnum GMs - meaning that when you were talking to that GM in game, you were usually talking to this character.

## Ashe

**Code Name:** Sierra
**Species:** Unknown, assumed human, vampiric 
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Deceased
**Fate:** Assassinated by a brainwashed [[#Kastia Talshen]] during the events of So Below.
GM avatar for Luna

**Description**
The leader of Eidolon and a vampiric creature of origins unknown. She was a major ally of the Riftborn and a powerful combatant. 

She was killed by Kastia, who had been brainwashed by [[C1 Antagonists#The Hierophant|The Hierophant]].

**Appearance**
Tan skin, blonde hair, typically wore military gear and was always armed.
## Cecil

**Code Name:** Mirage
**Species:** Human
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Status:** Unknown
GM avatar for Phil

**Description**
A leading operative of Eidolon and tech-savvy former mechanist and explosives expert. He was at one point imprisoned in The Blank Room, before being rescued by Eidolon. 

**META NOTE:** Conflicting Table Info - some tables saw Cecil be killed in one of the later narrative events (possibly So Below), but this hasn't been confirmed. He may have survived the events of Masquerade.

**Appearance**
Young man, about 20 years old, with pale skin and curly red hair.
# Helena
**Code Name:** Valentine
**Species:** Elf
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Alive as of the end of Campaign 1
**Location:** Unknown
GM avatar for Jenn

**Description**
[[#Ashe]]'s former therapist, a witch from Lumeria, and a leading operative of Eidolon, specialising in magic and the manipulation of the Astral Corridor. During the events of Lights Out, the Riftborn were forced to vote for one of their handlers, resulting in Helena being repeatedly murdered by [[C1 Antagonists#The Hierophant|The Hierophant]], then brought back to life, so many times that it left her functionally braindead. During the events of The Witch Queen, the Riftborn took Helena back to Lumeria and her mother, where she was revived.

**Appearance**
Long brown hair, brown skin, tattoos
## Kastia Talshen

**Code Name:** Wraith
**Species:** Tiefling
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Alive as of the end of Campaign 1
**Location:** Unknown
GM avatar for Gilli

**Description**
A leading operative of Eidolon and master of stealth. She was a major ally of the Riftborn, and sister to Ves Talshen. 

She survived the events of Masquerade, which ended with Kastia's marriage to Lysithea, then-Guild Leader of the Justicars.

**Appearance**
Red Tiefling with one broken horn, short curly black hair, covered in battle scars, wore black leather and always carried an amount of blades.
## Magnus

**Code Name:** Rook
**Species:** Unknown, assumed Human
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Status:** Alive as of the end of Masquerade
**Location:** Unknown
GM avatar for Maxim

**Description**
A leading operative of Eidolon, specialising in the social graces and political mechanisms of Adrestia. He survived the events of Masquerade.

**Appearance**
Well dressed gentleman of leisure. 

# Sinclair

**Code Name:** Gladius
**Species:** Unknown
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Status:** Alive as of the end of Masquerade
**Location:** Unknown
GM avatar for Tibet

**Description**
A source of chaotic energy and a seemingly endless supply of drugs. During moments of clarity, he was fiercely intelligent, with skills in weapons technology, explosives, hazardous chemical substances, and anything else he put his mind to. Outside of those moments, he was a drugged up mad man with access to all the same weapons technology, explosives, and hazardous chemical substances. He survived the events of Masquerade.

**Appearance**
Late 20s, slender build, black chin length hair that is usually slicked back. Criminal moustache. Dark grey eyes. Wears the latest and most extravagant fashions.
