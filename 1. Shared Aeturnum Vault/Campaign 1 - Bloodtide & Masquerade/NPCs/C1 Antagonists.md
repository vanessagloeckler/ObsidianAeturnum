# Major Antagonists
## Elias Jassen

**Species:** Unconfirmed, assumed human
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Status:** Deceased

Chairman of [[Adrestia#Aldwych Academy|Aldwych Academy]] and a member of [[C1 Lore#The Nine|The Nine]]. He was the puppet master behind many of the events of Campaign 1, and was also responsible for recovering [[C1 Lore#Riftborn|The Riftborn]], locking them into contracts that required them to serve him.

He was killed at the end of Campaign 1.
## The Hierophant

**Species:** Unconfirmed, assumed human
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Deceased

The main antagonist of the Masquerade arc, and sister of Ember. She sought revenge against Elias Jassen for what he and the Nine had done to Ember. She also wanted to seize the power of The Witness/Jacquetta Iverson for herself, in order to erase and rebuild reality in her own image. 

She was killed (multiple times) by the end of Aeturnum 1.
# Minor Antagonists
## Ves Talshen

**Species:** Half-Tiefling
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Current Status:** Unknown

Minor antagonist of the Riftborn during the events of Aeturnum 1, and sister of [[EIDOLON Agents#Kastia Talshen|Kastia Talshen]], an Eidolon operative and major player of the Masquerade arc. She was separated from/abandoned by Kastia as a child, and fell into a life of crime, fighting, and drugs in the [[Adrestia#The Undercity|Adrestian Undercity]]. She made an enemy of Speaker Zarissa, who was at the time the Syndicate crime boss. She eventually crossed paths with the Hierophant, who abducted and brainwashed her into fighting against Eidolon.  

A character matching Ves's distinct appearance appeared in [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis (Prelude)|Prelude]], the launch event of Campaign 2.
