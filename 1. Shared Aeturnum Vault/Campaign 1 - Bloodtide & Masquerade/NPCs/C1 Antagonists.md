# Major Antagonists
## Elias Jassen

**Species:** Unconfirmed, assumed human
**Status:** Deceased

Chairman of [[Adrestia#Aldwych Academy|Aldwych Academy]] and a member of [[C1 Lore#The Nine|The Nine]]. He was the puppet master behind many of the events of Campaign 1, and was also responsible for recovering [[C1 Lore#Riftborn|The Riftborn]], locking them into contracts that required them to serve him.

He was killed at the end of Campaign 1.
## The Hierophant

**Species:** Unconfirmed, assumed human
**Status:** Deceased

The main antagonist of the Masquerade arc, and sister of Ember. She sought revenge against Elias Jassen for what he and the Nine had done to Ember. She also wanted to seize the power of The Witness/Jacquetta Iverson for herself, in order to erase and rebuild reality in her own image. 

She was killed (multiple times) by the end of Aeturnum 1.
