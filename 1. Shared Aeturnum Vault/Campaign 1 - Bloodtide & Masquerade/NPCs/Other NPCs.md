# Lilah

**Species:** Unknown
**Status:** Deceased(?)

A young flower girl that was met by the Riftborn multiple times. During the events of Nightfall, she was killed and subsequently erased from history, existing only in the memories of the Riftborn. 

At the end of Masquerade, many who had been erased from time seemed to return, though it is unknown if Lilah is among their number.
