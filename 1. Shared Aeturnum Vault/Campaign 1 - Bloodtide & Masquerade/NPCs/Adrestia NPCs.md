# Tia
**Species:** Unknown
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Deceased(?)
**Location:** Unknown
**Role:** Member of [[Adrestia#Major Guilds The Mechanists|The Mechanist's Guild]]

The ward of [[Elaric Valent]]. She had some manner of deeply emotional attachment to Elaric, but was erased from existence and Elaric's memory when she was killed during the events of the Nightfall narrative event.

At the end of Masquerade, many who had been erased from time seemed to return, though it is unknown if Tia is among their number.
