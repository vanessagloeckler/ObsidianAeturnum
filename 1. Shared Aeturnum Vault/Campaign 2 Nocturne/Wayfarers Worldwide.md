Below is a list of The Wayfarers - the player characters from Campaign 2 - recorded here for posterity. 

Nathan has uploaded an initial list of all the PCs that they know of with basic stats. If you would like to contribute details about your character, or other characters you can remember vividly, please send us a message and we'll add your details. If you would like your character removed entirely, please let us know and we'll remove it. 

For the moment, we're keeping it limited to basic information, but as the story progresses (and more Wayfarers die), we can start adding a bit more detail in line with [[The Riftborn, Remembered]]. 

For the sake of privacy, we will not include the names or details of any player.
