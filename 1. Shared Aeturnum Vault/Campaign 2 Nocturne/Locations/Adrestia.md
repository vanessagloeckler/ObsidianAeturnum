# Basics

Adrestia, located on the continent of Esos, is the starting point of the Wayfarer's journey across the Red Isles, and it was once the home of all PCs.

It was also the primary setting for the events of campaign 1. 
# Districts
## Brigmore Commons

A working class district in the west of Adrestia, filled with workshops and workhouses, supply stores for tradespeople, and taverns. 
## The Foundry

An industrial district, kept separate from the rest of Adrestia with access heavily guarded via the Century Bridge. Its current status is unknown - a large chunk of it got blown up during the events of Masquerade in campaign 1. 
## The Silver District

At the time of Aeturnum 1, The Silver District was the wealthier part of town, where nobility and celebrities lived. Highgarden Court was located here, which contains a cathedral. Its current status is unknown.
## The Undercity

At the time of Aeturnum 1, The Undercity was a crime-rampant part of the city where people live in poverty, with illegal businesses such as fighting pits and drug trades operating with relative freedom.

As of the time of Aeturnum 2, [[The Speakers of Adrestia#Lady Zarissa|Lady Zarissa]], formerly of the crime organisation Syndicate, has become the Speaker of the Undercity, and the districts fortunes are on the rise.
## The Whaling District

An industry district on the harbour, in the east of Adrestia, where many factories and warehouses can be found.  
# Other Locations

## Aldwych Academy

A facility located on an island to the southeast of Adrestia. It was a core location during campaign 1, with every player character passing through it during their prologue. The current status of the Academy is unknown.
## Ivandara Temple

A temple located in the mountains above Adrestia, where statues of the heroic and forgotten founders of Adrestia are honoured. The [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis|Genesis expedition]] was dispatched from here.

# Culture
## First Blade & First Shield

Ceremonial titles, identifying major commanding officers of the forces of Adrestia.

Whether these titles still exist, and who holds them, is presently unknown. 
### First Blade

During the events of Aeturnum 1, this title was held by [[Wayfarer NPCs#Elaric Valent|Elaric Valent]].

Other title holders:
- Cassandra - Guildmaster of the Justicars during campaign 1 (assumedly succeeded Elaric)
- [[Other NPCs#Eris Pentaghast|Eris Pentaghast]] (succeeded Cassandra)
### First Shield

During the events of Aeturnum 1, this title was held by a man named Galadrin.
