# Basics

The northern most continent of the Red Isles, inhabited by vampires and ruled by [[Antagonists#Haera Agelamorn|Haera Agelamorn]].
# Aernoc & Aernoc Palace

The capital of Tisis and the seat of Haera Agelamorn.  
## Foundry

Located close to Aernoc Palace, the Foundry is an industrial complex that harvests blood from humanoids and pumps it into Aernoc to feed Haera's forces, while also pumping vampiric blood back into the victims, converting them into Bloodkin.
