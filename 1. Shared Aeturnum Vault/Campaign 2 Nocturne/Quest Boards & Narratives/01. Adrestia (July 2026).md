# Event Page

Your journey to the end of the world begins deep within the cloying smog, and rune-marked battlements of your former home. 

Adrestia.

In this chapter, the following Quests are available to you: 

**DISINTEGRATION:** Clear out a daemonic infestation plaguing the Undercity, and enlist the aid of an unlikely ally to uncover its true source. 

**HEARTS OF STONE:** Help one of Adrestia’s most controversial Speakers determine which of her subordinates are already ravenous vampiric thralls. 

**THE WAGER:** Resolve a high-stakes hostage situation before a retinue of lethal automata descend and turn the scene into a bloodbath. 

**ANATHEMA:** Track down a rogue vampiric alchemist stalking the Silver District and force them to formulate a remedy for the Petrification Sickness.
