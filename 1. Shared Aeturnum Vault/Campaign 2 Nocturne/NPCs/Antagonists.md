# Major Antagonists

These are the primary antagonists of Nocturne.

## Haera Agelamorn

**Species:** Vampire (mortal species, if she ever had one, is unknown)
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Alive
**Location:** Unknown, assumedly Aernoc

The Big Bad. Haera Agelamorn is the vampiric queen of Tisis, and the person responsible for the opening of the Worldwound and the petrification curse. She is also the source of the curseblood plague, that has created a psychic link between her and all that it has inflicted. 

During campaign 1, Haera was the subject of a three part mini-series called 'Severance'.

**Physical Appearance**
Deeply unnatural. Tall, blonde/silver hair, pale to the point of being almost white. Wears slender, silky clothes.
## The Five Lieutenants

The Five Lieutenants were five chosen among the victims of [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis (Prologue)|The Genesis Expedition]] to become the commanders of Haera's forces. They have not yet reappeared but will assumedly feature as we move through the campaign.

**Meta Note:** During Genesis, one PC was selected from each table to become a Lieutenant.
# Minor Antagonists

## The Warden & The Witch

The Warden & The Witch was a boss enemy encountered during [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis (Prologue)|The Genesis Expedition]]. The Witch was a spellcaster, capable of casting a shield that could block out the sun in a localised area. She was chained to the Warden, a strong and monstrously large humanoid that focused on keeping the heroes away from the Witch. 
# Minions

Below is a list of the recurring mob-enemies that we encounter. They don't represent any specific NPC, just stat-block creatures.
## Bloodkin

Grotesque undead monsters with 'too many teeth'. They are small and easy to kill, especially with fire, but are near unstoppable in a hoard, and are capable of inflicting the Haemorrhage condition.
## Genesis Enemies

The following is a list of enemies that we encountered during Genesis:
- Bloodkin-esque Monsters (Minions)
- Bloodshot Archers
- Vampiric Commander
- Vampiric Knights
## Wraiths

Unknown, ghostlike creatures whose wailing shriek causes bloodkin to go into a frenzy.
