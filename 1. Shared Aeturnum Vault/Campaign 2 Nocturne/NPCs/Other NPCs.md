# Eris Pentaghast
**Species:** Unknown, assumed human
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Status:** Unknown
**Location:** Unknown

Eris Pentaghast was a major NPC during the events of [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis (Prologue)|Genesis]]. She was at the time the First Blade of [[Adrestia]], and was responsible for leading the attack on Aernoc Palace. As of the end of Genesis, she was alive, but imprisoned by [[Antagonists#Haera Agelamorn|Haera]]. Her current status is unknown.
