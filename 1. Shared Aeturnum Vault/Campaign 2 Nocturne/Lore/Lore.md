# Curses

There are currently two separate, but related curses ravaging the world.
## Curseblood Plague

This is the name of the vampiric plague that exists within many of the remaining living people in the world, including all player characters. Overtime, it weakens a person and causes them to lose their humanity, slowly converting them into vampiric creatures such as bloodkin. It also links us all directly to [[Antagonists#Haera Agelamorn|Haera Agelamorn]], and is the reason we have lost all but one of our memories. 
## Petrification Curse

A curse connected directly to the [[#Worldwound]]. It is causing people, animals, plant life, and seemingly all organic matter to slowly calcify and turn to stone. There is no known pattern to who gets affected by it and who doesn't, but it will eventually impact all organic life as the Worldwound grows larger.
# The Herald

It's presently unknown who or what the Herald is. It was referenced by [[The Speakers of Adrestia]] at the time of [[00. Prelude, Prologue, Primer#Genesis (Prelude)|Genesis]]. "The Herald is coming, fear the night."
# The Outer God of Blood

AKA: The Arch God of Blood, The Fell God.

A deity worshipped by [[Antagonists#Haera Agelamorn|Haera Agelamorn]]. 

It previously featured in the Campaign 1 arc 'Bloodtide', where it was responsible for the creation of the Bloodkin.
# Worldwound

A void that has appeared above The Red Isles, as a result of something that [[Antagonists#Haera Agelamorn|Haera Agelamorn]] did 30 years ago. It is growing, and covering the world in darkness, allowing the vampiric hoards to roam free. It is described as a void in the sky, surrounded by red and purple motes of energy. It is connected to the [[#Petrification Curse]].
